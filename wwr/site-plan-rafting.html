Overview

Purpose

You'll find gallons of fun at Splash White Water Rafting. Our purpose is to give you and your family a fun and safe experience, enjoying the views and the water!

Audience

1. Who are the target customers? Our target customers are middle class people who want to have fun and a safe experience. People older than 5 years old can practice this sport.

2. What are people looking for? There are people looking for a relaxing journey down the river, they are more interested in the beauty of the land/water around them, rather than the excitement of white water rapids. Large groups looking for a funny activity which all members of the group can practice (Example: A family). An individual looking for something extreme and funny. Something that he hasen't tried before. Last but not least, all of them are looking for a safe experience.

3. How will people access at the site? Laptop Tablet Mobile Devices
